📚 Definitions

Maximum Residue Limit (MRL): The highest legally permitted concentration of pesticides or veterinary drugs allowed in food.

Below the Limit of Quantification (BLQ): Indicates a substance (like contaminants, pesticides, or toxins) is present at such a low level that it cannot be accurately measured by the laboratory equipment, generally signifying high food safety.
