# ChoosePure Brand Guidelines

## Colors
- Pure Ivory: #FAFAF7 (backgrounds)
- Deep Leaf Green: #1F6B4E (primary, CTAs, headers)
- Grain Brown: #8A6E4B (secondary, borders)
- Lab Grey: #6B6B6B (body text, data)
- Mint: #95DB93 (highlights, success)
- Warning Amber: #FFB703 (test warnings ONLY)
- Fail Red: #D62828 (test failures ONLY)

## Typography
- Primary: Inter (400, 500, 600)
- Data: Roboto Mono (400, 500)
- H1: 32px/2rem, H2: 24px/1.5rem, H3: 20px/1.25rem
- Body: 14-16px, line-height 1.5
- Headings: line-height 1.2, letter-spacing -0.02em

## Components
- Buttons: 12px 24px padding, 8px radius
- Cards: white bg, 1px solid #E5E5E5, 12px radius, shadow 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Inputs: 12px 16px padding, 8px radius, 1px solid #D1D1D1
- 60-30-10 rule: 60% Pure Ivory, 30% Deep Leaf Green, 10% accents

## Voice
- Calm, conversational, data-backed
- Lead with facts, not fear
- Empowerment over guilt
