1. Nature of ChoosePure Services

ChoosePure is an independent, community-driven consumer awareness platform dedicated to food quality, purity, nutrition, and processing transparency. We operate without brand sponsorship or commercial influence from any food manufacturer.

  • All testing is facilitated through NABL-accredited laboratories following FSSAI, FDA, and EFSA protocols and methodologies.
  • We share lab reports and simplified insights to help consumers make informed choices about the food they consume.
  • ChoosePure does not manufacture, sell, distribute, or endorse any food product.
  • ChoosePure acts solely as an intermediary between consumers and accredited testing laboratories, funded entirely by community contributions and subscriptions.
  • No brand, manufacturer, or commercial entity has any editorial control or sponsorship influence over our testing process, results, or publications.

By using ChoosePure, you expressly acknowledge and agree that:

  • All food testing is conducted in NABL-accredited laboratories using standard procedures aligned with FSSAI, FDA, and EFSA protocols.
